STEP1- Prepare your materials and ingredients: Gather tools: measuring cups and spoons, an electric mixer (or whisk), a spatula, and an 8-inch cake pan.

STEP2- Measure the dry ingredients: measure and whisk together 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour, 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder, and ¼ teaspoon salt.

STEP3-Soften the butter: Let ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter sit at room temperature until soft. This makes it easier to mix and results in a light, airy texture for your cake.

STEP4- Cream the butter and sugar: In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the softened butter with 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar. resulting in a soft, tender texture.

STEP5- Add eggs one at a time: that the eggs blend smoothly into the butter mixture without curdling. Stir in 1 ½ teaspoons of vanilla extract for flavor.

STEP6- Alternate dry ingredients and milk: Measure ½ cup (120ml) whole milk. Add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture in three parts, alternating with the milk.

STEP7- Mix until just combined: Use a spatula to gently fold the batter after mixing to make sure all ingredients are evenly combined.

STEP8-Transfer the batter to the cake pan: Pour the batter into your prepared cake pan. Use the spatula to spread it evenly across the pan, smoothing the top to ensure even baking.

STEP9- Bake the cake: Place the pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes. the end of baking time to avoid over-baking, which can dry out the cake.

STEP10- Cool the cake: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before carefully turning it out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
