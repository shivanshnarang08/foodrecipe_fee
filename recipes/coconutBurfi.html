Pink Coconut Burfi Recipe

By Sankalp Prashar

★★★★☆


Recipe Tags : Dessert Sweet Dish

Cooking Time

60 - 90 Minutes

Servings

4

Difficulty Level

Medium - Difficult

Category

Dessert



Step1

Heat a pan and add coconut and milk. Stir well until combined and bring the mixture to a boil.

Step2

Add jaggery and mix well. Cook until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.

Step3

Add condensed milk and mix well. Keep stirring until the mixture changes color and forms a lump.

Step4

Heat a non-stick pan and add coconut, sugar, fresh cream, and green cardamom powder. Cook, stirring continuously, until the mixture turns golden brown.

Step5

Put oil in the pot at least 10 cm high and heat the oil to 200°C over medium high heat.

Step6

For 1 min fry the fries and then wait for then put it into the freezer for 20 min to cool down

Step7

Then Repeat Fry it until it turns golden.

Step8

Leave the fries for 30 min to cool.

Step9

Sprinkle the salt and Seasoning of your choice and then toss it to mix and Serve!










Ingredient-1

Freshly Grated Coconut

2 cups

Ingredient-2

Condensed Milk

Ingredient-3

Sugar: 1/2 cup

Salt , Garlic powder , perri perri masala, black pepper , paprika.

Ingredient-4

Cardamom Powder: 1/2 tsp

Nuts: Cashews and almonds, chopped, for garnish
