Dosa Recipe

By Tania

★★★★☆


Recipe Tags : Lunch Dinner Snack

Cooking Time

30 Minutes

Servings

1

Difficulty Level

Easy

Category

South Indian



Step1

Soak: Soak the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately in water for at least 6 hours.p>

Step2

Grind: Grind the soaked ingredients separately to a smooth paste using water as needed, then mix them together. Add salt.

Step3

Ferment: Allow the batter to ferment overnight or for about 8-12 hours until it becomes bubbly and rises.

Step4

Heat a non-stick pan and add coconut, sugar, fresh cream, and green cardamom powder. Cook, stirring continuously, until the mixture turns golden brown.

Step5

Put oil in the pot at least 10 cm high and heat the oil to 200°C over medium high heat.

Step6

For 1 min fry the fries and then wait for then put it into the freezer for 20 min to cool down

Step7

Then Repeat Fry it until it turns golden.

Step8

Leave the fries for 30 min to cool.

Step9

Sprinkle the salt and Seasoning of your choice and then toss it to mix and Serve!










Ingredient-1

Rice

1 cup

Ingredient-2

Urad Dal (Black Gram)

1/2 cup

Ingredient-3

Ginger-Garlic Paste

2 tbsp

Ingredient-4

Cardamom Powder: 1/2 tsp

Nuts: Cashews and almonds, chopped, for garnish
